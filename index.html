Buyers' Specialists in Southbay Real Estate

The Southbay area is a vibrant bunch of coastal and inland cities in Southern California. Every community is different and offers a variety of lifestyle options. From fine dining and beach side living in Manhattan Beach, to the surf and sand sports of Hermosa Beach, to the bustling international port of San Pedro and the magnificent sweeping views of Palos Verdes Estates, there is something for everyone and anyone who wishes to move to the area.

Sedgewick Real Estate Company is a small boutique real estate firm that stands ready to help in this quest. We are a small team of agents that live and work in the area and know it's history and unique attributes intimately. We specialize in buyers' services and can help you find the home of your dreams. Whether it's a weekend beach getaway, a magnificent mansion with sweeping ocean views or a forever family home. We stand ready to serve. Call us today!
